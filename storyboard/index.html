⬤ Act I — The Encounter
SCENE 01 00:00 – 00:18  |  18s
Wide Establishing Shot — Rainy Alley
EXTREME WIDE STATIC → SLOW PUSH IN 28mm WIDE RAIN VFX · FOG
Two figures face each other in a rain-soaked back alley. Street lamp creates hard rim lighting. Hero left, villain right. Camera holds static then slowly dollies forward over 10 seconds.
▶Camera: STATIC → slow dolly push (5–10% zoom)
▶Rain particles: top-to-bottom, slight angle wind-right
▶Coat fabric: subtle wind ripple simulation
♫
rain ambience · distant thunder · tense low drone

ECU Hero Face — Low Angle Lock
EXTREME CLOSE-UP STATIC 85mm PORTRAIT RIM LIGHT · BOKEH BG
Extreme close-up of the hero's face. Camera positioned just below eye-line (low angle). Rim light from street lamp catches jaw and temple. Eyes lock on threat. Rain drops on skin.
▶Camera: LOCKED — no movement, holds tension
▶Micro-expression: jaw tightens, pupils dilate
▶Rain drops: slide down face surface in real-time
♫
heartbeat sub-bass · silence swells
SCENE 03 00:30 – 00:45  |  15s
Two-Shot — Villain Advances
MEDIUM TWO-SHOT SLOW LATERAL TRACK → 50mm NORMAL ENERGY VFX · PURPLE AURA
Villain steps forward with glowing hand extended. Hero retreats one step. Camera tracks laterally right at waist height, maintaining both figures in frame. Purple energy crackles from villain's fist.
▶Camera: slow lateral dolly right (tracking villain advance)
▶VFX: energy orb pulses at 2Hz, tendrils extend toward hero
▶Cloth physics: villain cloak billows backward from movement
♫
electrical hum · villain voice distortion
⬤ Act II — The Fight
SCENE 04 00:45 – 01:05  |  20s
ACTION — First Punch Impact
MEDIUM SHOT HANDHELD SHAKE 35mm IMPACT FLASH · SPEED LINES
Hero launches first strike — a right cross that connects with villain's jaw. Frame freezes for 2 frames on impact. Speed lines radiate from fist. Camera shakes on contact. Villain's head snaps left.
▶Camera: handheld micro-shake on impact frame (2-frame hold)
▶Hero punch: motion blur trail (3-frame smear) leading into fist
▶Villain head: snap rotation 45° right over 0.1s then settle
♫
bone crack · whoosh · impact thud · music spike
SCENE 05 01:05 – 01:20  |  15s
Overhead Crane — Spinning Kick
BIRD'S-EYE / OVERHEAD SLOW ROTATE (CRANE) 24mm WIDE (DRONE) MOTION ARC · SLOW-MO 60fps
Crane / drone shot looking straight down. Hero performs spinning back kick. Camera rotates 30° clockwise in sync with hero's spin — matching the rotation energy. Slowed to 60fps for clarity.
▶Camera: overhead, slow clockwise rotation (0.5 rot/sec)
▶Hero spin: 360° body rotation, kick lands at 270° mark
▶Slow-mo: 60fps playback → 40% speed gives float effect
♫
slow-pitch whoosh · reverb kick · time stretch sfx
SCENE 06 01:20 – 01:35  |  15s
Hero Throws Villain into Wall
MEDIUM WIDE WHIP PAN → SNAP ZOOM 50mm DEBRIS · WALL CRACK · DUST
Hero grabs villain and hurls them into a concrete wall. Camera whip-pans right to follow the throw, then snap-zooms in on villain impact. Wall cracks radiate from impact point. Dust cloud bursts outward.
▶Camera: whip pan right (0.15s) → snap zoom to wall (0.1s)
▶Wall debris: physics sim — 8–12 brick chunks fly outward
▶Dust: volumetric burst from impact point, settles over 1.5s
♫
concrete smash · rubble scatter · echo decay
SCENE 07 01:35 – 01:50  |  15s
Villain Counter — Energy Blast
MEDIUM WIDE DUTCH TILT 15° 35mm ENERGY BEAM · SHOCKWAVE
Villain recovers and fires a twin-handed energy blast. Camera holds a Dutch tilt (15°) to convey disorientation. Hero is hit — stumbles backward. Shockwave ripples expand from contact point.
▶Camera: 15° Dutch tilt, locked — heightens dread
▶Beam VFX: linear travel L→R, 0.05s delay after hands charge
▶Hero: pushed back 2m, coat shreds at blast edge
♫
energy charge whine → blast boom → silence spike

EXTREME LOW ANGLE STATIC → SLOW TILT UP 18mm ULTRA WIDE AURA GLOW · GROUND DUST
Camera placed at ground level. Hero rises from fallen position — filling the frame from feet to sky. Slow tilt up from boots to face over 4 seconds. Red energy aura radiates outward. Empowering beat drop.

360° Orbit — Final Clash
360° ORBIT SHOT FULL ORBIT 4 SEC 35mm CLASH EXPLOSION · FREEZE FRAME
Camera orbits 360° around both fighters as their fists meet in a final clash. The orbit takes exactly 4 seconds. At the moment of impact (180° mark), time freezes for 1 full second — then explodes back to real speed.
▶Camera: 360° full orbit at constant speed, pivot = midpoint
▶Time: freeze on impact (1s hold) → fast-forward burst back to RT
▶Explosion: radial shockwave, debris orbit with camera path
♫
time stop whoom → silence → massive explosion crack
SCENE 10 — FINALE 02:18 – 02:30  |  12s

MEDIUM WIDE SLOW DOLLY BACK 85mm PORTRAIT MOONLIGHT SHAFT · FADE
Hero stands tall over defeated villain. Single moonlight shaft breaks through cloud and illuminates the hero. Camera slowly dollies backward over 8 seconds, making the hero appear smaller against the night sky. Fade to black.

🎥 CAMERA MOVES
DOLLY Physical push in/out on rails
WHIP PAN Fast horizontal rotation
SNAP ZOOM Rapid optical zoom burst
ORBIT 360° arc around subject
CRANE Vertical rise / overhead
DUTCH TILT Camera canted ~15° for dread
⚡ ANIMATION TECHNIQUES
SMEAR Motion-blur frame smear on fast hits
FREEZE 2-frame hold on max-impact
SLOW-MO 60fps → 40% playback speed
SPEED LINES Manga-inspired radial lines
CLOTH SIM Real-time fabric physics
PARTICLE Dust, debris, rain particles
